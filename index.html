Lesson 01

Jump Ball

In this lesson, I familiarized myself with the interface, tools, and workflow in Rive. I applied the basic techniques in a hands-on project to understand the fundamentals of working with Rive and build a solid foundation in motion design!

Hover over the bird, and it will change direction based on the cursor's position;
Click on the ball, and it will change its skin;
Click on the floor, and the ladder will turn into a road

Lesson 02

Water Bubble

In this lesson, I learned how to create realistic water effects. I discovered how to incorporate sliders to improve user interaction, and I applied these skills to simulate the natural, dynamic movement of water.

Hover your cursor over the ball and click on it.
Experiment with the ball's size and speed using the sliders.

Lesson 03

Belly Mixing

In this lesson, I configured the character rigging and joystick controls. I refined the animation controls, learned techniques for smooth transitions between states to create seamless movements, and learned how to create more complex and realistic character movements.

Use the slider and experiment with the amplitude.

Lesson 04

Sunny Rock

In this lesson, I learned more about advanced customization and personalization options, rigged a character, and studied mixing techniques in Rive to create diverse and unique animations with a professional look.

Make the Sunny dance rock.
Hover your cursor over it and click.
Experiment with the Sunny's size and amplitude using the sliders.
